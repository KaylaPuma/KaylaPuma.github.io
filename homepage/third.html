WELCOME TO GORMAN PLAYGROUND!

Look at these cool facts about the playground!

  • It is located in East Elmhurst, Queens!
  • It was founded in 1963 in memorial of Denis P. Gorman who helped with community organizations.
  • This playground is in between Astoria Boulevard and Northern Boulevard!
  • The neighborhood is famous for its Hispanic population!
  • The playground takes up a whole street block in the area!
  • Apart from the playground at the entrance, there is a handball and basketball court at the park.
  • There is also a tree area to relax, walk your dog, or play chess!
  • This spot is special for me because it is where I spent time with friends!
  • You can come here for ice cream and cool off in the summer!
  • During winter, come here to play in the snow and take nice pictures!
  • This park is great to just walk around and catch up with pals!
  • I spent a lot of time playing at this park throughout my childhood!
  • The people here are friendly and respect your space; we all come here to relax!
